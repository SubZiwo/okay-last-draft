What Is TVL-ICT?

TVL stands for Technical Vocation and Livelihood. and is designed to give students, specialized experience, and hands on experience The TVL-ICT Track is a program that helps students develop the technical skills needed, for employment in the ICT and IT sectors. The program is designed to give students hands-on experience with computers, software, and technology, while also preparing them for life after school. Students learn how to use computers and software programs, which can help them find jobs in an ICT or IT field.
